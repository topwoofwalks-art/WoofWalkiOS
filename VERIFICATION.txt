ALGORITHM VERIFICATION
----------------------

Livestock Suitability Formula:
score = (grass × 0.40) +
        (crops × 0.25) +
        (shrub × 0.15) +
        (trees × 0.10) +
        (built × -0.50) +
        (water × -0.30)

normalized = max(0, min(100, score × 100))

Rating Scale:
0-30   = Poor (Red)
30-50  = Fair (Orange)
50-70  = Good (Yellow)
70-100 = Excellent (Green)
